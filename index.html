WHO WE ARE

About the PBEN Network

The Pacific Biomedical Engineering Network (PBEN) operates as an expert regional body under the auspice of the Directors of Clinical Services (DCS) and Pacific Heads of Health (HoH).

PBEN's main objective is to provide a comprehensive, dedicated platform for the continuous improvement of regional coordination, clinical quality standards, capacity development, and technology life-cycle management.

Our Core Commitment

To provide Pacific Island Countries and Territories (PICTs) total ownership of, and continuous means to input into, a standardized biomedical engineering services framework designed from within the region.

SPC Strategic Plan KFA 6: Planetary Health

Aligning biomedical technology choices to ecological limits and environmental resilience.

2050 Blue Pacific Strategy Goal 1: Health & Well-being

Ensuring healthy societies across all Pacific peoples by improving clinical assets.

United Nations SDG SDG 3: Healthy Lives

Promoting well-being and life-saving clinical uptime across remote hospitals.

Our Core Values

U

We progress together synchronously. No island, technician, or hospital is left behind.

We continuously strive for the highest international standard of safe clinical engineering calibration.

I

Innovation (Adaptive Tech)
