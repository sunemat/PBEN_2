Who We Are

Strengthening Clinical Resilience Across PICTs

The **Pacific Biomedical Engineering Network (PBEN)** forum operates directly under the guidance of the **Directors of Clinical Services (DCS)** and the **Pacific Heads of Health (HoH)**.

Core Objective of the Forum

PBEN provides a sovereign platform for the improvement of **coordination, collaboration, and capacity development** of its members. The overarching goal is to grant Pacific Island Countries & Territories (PICTs) full ownership of, and a collective mechanism to deliver inputs into, a regional biomedical engineering services development platform for the Pacific.

Standardizing policies, training, and maintenance frameworks.

Our Sacred Values

Foundational principles steering our biomedical network across boundaries.

Aroha (Care)

We deeply care about our communities. We take full, active ownership of our Biomedical Engineering work.

Unity

We progress together as a unified region. We leave no nation and no island community behind.

Excellence

We strive to be the absolute best. We mandate, practice, and deliver premium clinical safety standards.

Innovation

We continuously improve, stay resourceful, and evolve synchronously with rapid technological changes.

Our Vision

“Advanced biomedical engineering services for a healthier Blue Pacific”

Imagining a fully unified healthcare technology landscape across our expansive Pacific Ocean continent.

Our Mission

“To deliver innovative and sustainable biomedical engineering services in the Pacific by strengthening capacity building, policy development, regional collaboration and promotion of technologies.”

Guiding daily regional and national practices with operational standards and professional pathways.

Strategic Goals

“To strengthen biomedical engineering services in the Blue Pacific by ensuring equitable access to well-maintained medical equipment, supported by a qualified and competent biomedical workforce”

Our metric of success remains direct access of regional hospitals to sustainable diagnostics.
