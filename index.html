About the PBEN Network

The Pacific Biomedical Engineering Network (PBEN) serves as the premier coordination forum for clinical engineering technologies and clinical system maintenance across all Pacific nations.

Governed by the Directors of Clinical Services (DCS) and Pacific Heads of Health (HoH), PBEN coordinates regional technology sharing, professional capacity building, standardized equipment policies, and responsive emergency medical technology surge frameworks.

Our Regional Blueprint

By managing equipment cycles inside a customized local sandbox, we ensure that no island country is left behind when maintaining life-saving ICU, emergency, and primary care assets.

SPC Strategic Plan KFA 6: Planetary Health

Matching biomedical assets with regional eco-health, sustainable energy, and clean waste systems.

2050 Strategy Goal 1: Blue Pacific Health

Increasing life span and general wellness of Pacific peoples via reliable medical tech uptime.

United Nations SDG SDG 3: Healthy Lives

Guaranteeing healthy lives and promoting universal healthcare quality at all island ages.

Vision

"Advanced biomedical engineering services for a healthier Blue Pacific"

Mission

"To deliver innovative and sustainable biomedical engineering services in the Pacific by strengthening capacity building, policy development, regional collaboration and promotion of technologies."

Goals

"To strengthen biomedical engineering services in the Blue Pacific by ensuring equitable access to well-maintained medical equipment, supported by a qualified and competent biomedical workforce"

Our Core Values

A

Aroha (Care)

We care profoundly about our community health networks and take complete, local ownership of our biomedical workflows.

U

Unity (Leaving No One Behind)

We navigate together synchronously across the ocean. No remote island clinic or hospital technician is left behind.

E

Excellence (Quality Standards)

We strive to be the absolute best and provide a verified, safe, and quality clinical standard of equipment care.

I

Innovation (Evolving tech)

We continuously improve, stay resourceful, and evolve flexibly with changes in green biomedical technologies.

Our 6 Strategic Objectives

Objective 01

Advocacy & Awareness

Advocate for governments (through MOHs), donor partners and regional support, to prioritise and support BME in the Blue Pacific.

Standardized campaign models
Objective 02

Workforce Development

Ensure a qualified, competent and dedicated BME workforce in the Blue Pacific. Standardize technician-to-bed density.

Minimum Qualifications Standard
Objective 03

Education & Training

Facilitate and improve access to formal BME education, attachments, mentorships, and certified training in the Blue Pacific.

Regional CPD framework active
Objective 04

Data and Research

Strengthen collection, research, and technical telemetry monitoring databases to guide long-term equipment acquisition decisions.

Dashboard analytics database
Objective 05

Institutional Strengthening

Support PICTs to strengthen and develop improved robust BME structures, policies, governance, and donation collaboration guidelines.

NMEM / MEMC Policy alignment
Objective 06

Monitoring & Evaluation

Use robust evaluation metrics, peer site inspections, and calibration logs to track the ongoing deployment of strategic network assets.

Annual coordination assessments
